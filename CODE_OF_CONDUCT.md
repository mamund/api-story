# Code of conduct

This project follows a simple principle: be constructive.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Keep discussions grounded in the goals of the repository.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Publishing private information

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior undermines a safe, constructive environment.
